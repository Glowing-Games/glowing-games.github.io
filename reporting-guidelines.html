Reporting Guidelines

How to Submit a Clear & Effective Report

These guidelines are for anyone submitting reports—such as bug reports, incident reports, or general feedback—to help our team resolve issues or suggestions efficiently.
  1. Title: A concise summary of the issue or topic.
  2. Description: Details, including:
    • What happened?
    • What did you expect?
    • When did it occur?
    • Who is affected?
  3. Steps to Reproduce (if applicable): List actions to trigger the issue.
  4. Supporting Evidence: Attach logs, screenshots, or code snippets if available.
  5. Environment: (e.g., browser, device, version, OS)
  6. Impact: How severe is the issue?
  • Be clear, specific, and objective.
  • Avoid jargon if possible.
  • Use bullet points or numbered lists for steps.
  • Use proper grammar and punctuation.
